"crash course" I am teaching in January and February of 2026. The
topic is first-order continuous optimization, with a focus on the
fundamentals for splitting algorithms and Frank-Wolfe algorithms.
These notes are not meant to be used as a standalone resource.

via e-mail.  If you would like feedback on the homework exercises,
please drop them off at my office (Roop 310).
